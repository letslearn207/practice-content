================================================================================
                              BONUS TIPS
================================================================================

General Preparation Tips:
- Be ready to explain real-world scenarios and use cases
- Practice hands-on labs (create clusters, deploy apps, troubleshoot)
- Understand not just "how" but also "why" behind each concept
- Be familiar with recent trends: GitOps, Service Mesh, Serverless
- Prepare examples from your own projects
- Understand security best practices across all topics
- Know common DevOps tools: Jenkins, ArgoCD, Prometheus, ELK Stack, etc.

For 3-4 Years Experience:
- You're expected to handle production issues independently
- Know infrastructure-as-code concepts (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Understand CI/CD pipeline design and implementation
- Be familiar with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Know monitoring, logging, and alerting strategies
- Understand capacity planning and cost optimization
